What is the GR III Street Edition? ▶

 

The Ricoh GR III Street Edition is a bold, limited-release version of the GR III, made for street photographers who move with purpose and shoot with instinct. Its unique asphalt-like textured body and standout orange-yellow lens ring reflect the energy and unpredictability of the urban landscape. Every detail is designed to blend performance with attitude.




Key Features ▶

 

- 24.2MP APS-C sensor with stunning detail and tone
- 28mm equivalent f/2.8 GR lens ideal for street capture
- Asphalt-gray finish with orange ring cap
- Fast hybrid AF and responsive snap focus
- Built-in ND filter and in-body stabilization
- Compact, discreet body for low-profile shooting
- Street-focused firmware extras like Full Press Snap
- Custom startup screen exclusive to the edition




Ideal Use Cases ▶

 

Purpose-built for spontaneous street photography, the GR III Street Edition is ideal for fast-paced urban scenes, candid people shots, and layered compositions. It excels when agility, discretion, and fast reaction are essential. Whether you're exploring alleys, riding subways, or documenting life on the move, it keeps you focused and ready.




Design & Extras ▶

 

The Street Edition stands out with its rugged, tactile asphalt-gray finish and bright orange ring cap that evokes traffic markings and city lights. It includes a special leather wrist strap and a custom metal viewfinder in select bundles. This is not just a camera—it’s a statement made for those who live in the moment and shoot accordingly.




Who is the Street Edition for? ▶

 

The GR III Street Edition is made for photographers who embrace speed, stealth, and spontaneity. Whether you're a lifelong street shooter or a traveler with a creative eye, this edition combines form and function with a sharp visual edge. It’s a collector-grade tool that’s meant to be used—anywhere and everywhere.
