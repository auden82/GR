The GR Community



What is the GR community? ▶

 

GR users are more than camera owners—they’re a community of passionate, observant photographers around the world. From street shooters to portrait artists, GR users share a respect for everyday moments, personal style, and the value of carrying a camera always. Whether online or in person, the GR community connects through a shared creative spirit.


What are GR Meetups? ▶

 

GR Meetups are informal photo walks and gatherings hosted by GR users and Ricoh Imaging. They take place in cities around the world and give photographers a chance to meet, shoot, share, and learn from each other. Whether you're new to the GR or a long-time user, it's a welcoming space to connect with others who see the world through a similar lens.


What are GR Cities? ▶

 

GR Cities is an initiative that highlights cities around the globe where the GR has become part of the street photography scene. These featured cities celebrate the photographers and styles that define local culture. Through exhibitions, city profiles, and artist features, GR Cities promotes photography as an ongoing dialogue between place and people.


Why does this matter? ▶

 

We don’t just sell cameras—we’re here to support a photographic lifestyle. The GR series has always been about more than specs: it's about cultivating how you see the world. Through community events, shared stories, and real-world photography, we aim to be part of your creative journey, not just a tool in your bag. Join the culture—not just the camera.
