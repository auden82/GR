What is the GR III Diary Edition? ▶

 

The Ricoh GR III Diary Edition blends the acclaimed imaging performance of the GR III with a warm, tactile aesthetic and limited-edition styling. Designed to turn everyday life into visual stories, it features a metallic gray finish, bronze accents, and an exclusive on-screen startup message—all tailored to the photographer who values personal storytelling and creative reflection.




Key Features ▶

 

- 24.2MP APS-C CMOS sensor for exceptional image quality
- 28mm equivalent f/2.8 GR lens with wide-angle clarity
- Stylish textured metallic gray body with bronze trim
- Hybrid autofocus with fast phase/contrast detection
- In-body image stabilization for low-light confidence
- 3” touchscreen LCD with intuitive operation
- USB-C charging and seamless wireless sharing
- Custom startup screen and leather-style ring cap (included)
