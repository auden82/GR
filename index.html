GR HISTORY


The GR: Always Real


The GR has never followed trends. No zoom lens, no flashy gimmicks—just a solid wide-angle prime that stays true to the craft. While others chase the next big thing, the GR evolves slowly, getting smarter with firmware, not facelifts.

Its look? Clean, simple, and purposeful. Each new model sticks to the roots. Why? Because it’s not about hype—it’s about photographers who actually care.

The GR’s story is built on real feedback from real users. It’s a low-key icon made by people who love what they do and built for those who just want to shoot.
