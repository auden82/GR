What is the Ricoh GR III? ▶

 

The Ricoh GR III is a premium compact camera engineered for creators who demand outstanding image quality without compromise on portability. With a large APS-C sensor, high-performance 28mm equivalent lens, and fast, responsive controls, it offers a perfect balance of power and discretion—ideal for capturing the world as you see it.




Key Features ▶

 

- 24.2MP APS-C CMOS sensor (no low-pass filter)
- 28mm equivalent f/2.8 GR lens with edge-to-edge sharpness
- Fast hybrid autofocus (phase + contrast detection)
- 3-axis in-body image stabilization (IBIS)
- Built-in ND filter for bright-light shooting
- Compact magnesium alloy body—lightweight yet robust
- Intuitive 3” touchscreen interface
- USB-C charging, built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth




Ideal Use Cases ▶

 

The GR III excels in street photography, travel, and everyday shooting. Its wide-angle lens is perfect for capturing the energy of urban life, scenic landscapes, and immersive environmental portraits. With close focusing capability down to 6cm, it’s also well-suited for still life, textures, and detail-rich compositions.




Customization and Controls ▶

 

Designed with photographers in mind, the GR III offers full control with customizable buttons, user modes, and snap focus for lightning-fast response. Its minimalist layout ensures an unobtrusive shooting experience, while the touchscreen adds quick navigation and ease of use.




Who is the GR III for? ▶

 

The Ricoh GR III is made for those who see the beauty in everyday moments. Whether you're a street photographer, visual storyteller, traveler, or creative looking for DSLR-quality images in a compact body, the GR III is your ideal everyday companion. It's for photographers who value simplicity, spontaneity, and excellence in every frame.
